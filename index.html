Our Editing

For reasonable prices, we are offering personalized brainstorming and revisions so that you can make the most out of your essay! See below for the specifics:

Offline Essay Editing (via Google Docs): Applicants can submit up to 5 revisions that our team will thoughtfully edit and provide advice on writing style, flow, story telling, and overall structure.

$35 for 100 words

Brainstorming (via Zoom):Applicants meet one-on-one with a member of our team to brainstorm ideas for essays and receive personalized feedback on how to most effectively answer the prompt while enhancing the application.

$15 for 20-minute meeting, $30 for 40-minute meeting

Revisions (via Zoom):Applicants can receive live advice from a member of our team to go through revisions and start making general changes together, and will also have the opportunity to ask questions about their writing or our own essays and statistics as former college applicants.

$15 for 20-minute meeting, $30 for 40-minute meeting.
